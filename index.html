Our practice

A calmer dental visit. Tucked inside a Pelham Road cottage.

When you walk in, you'll notice it doesn't feel like a dental office. That's intentional. Since 1985, Pelham Oaks has been built around one idea: great dentistry delivered in a space that feels personal, quiet, and low-pressure.

  • Low-stress for nervous patientsComfort options, blankets, and a front desk that actually remembers your name.
  • Digital X-rays & modern equipmentLow-dose imaging, intraoral cameras, and quiet chairs.
  • Easy parking · easy check-inOff-street lot, fill out new patient forms from your phone before you arrive.
What we do

Comprehensive care, from first tooth to final crown.

Every patient is treated like family. Dr. Morrow, Dr. Breaux, and Dr. Reardon cover the full scope of modern general dentistry in a warm, low-stress cottage setting.

Preventive Dentistry

Stay ahead of it. Cleanings, exams, digital X-rays, sealants, and fluoride for every age in the family.

  • Six-month cleanings & exams
  • Digital X-rays (low-dose)
  • Kids' sealants & fluoride
  • Gum-disease screening

Cosmetic & Restorative

Bring a confident smile back. Veneers, crowns, whitening, bonding, and tooth-colored fillings.

  • Porcelain veneers & crowns
  • In-office whitening
  • Tooth-colored fillings
  • Bridges & full smile makeovers

Surgical & Other Services

We handle the things other practices refer out. Extractions, root canals, implants, and emergency visits.

  • Wisdom tooth extractions
  • Root canal therapy
  • Dental implants
  • Same-day emergency visits
